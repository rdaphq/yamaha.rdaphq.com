At the heart of the MT-09 lies a potent 890cc CP3 engine, delivering instant torque and responsive acceleration that makes every twist of the throttle unforgettable. Meet "Master of Torque".

Experience next-level riding with the all-new Yamaha MT-09 2025 — Yamaha's iconic Hyper Naked, now with advanced Yamaha Automated Manual Transmission (Y-AMT). Combining raw torque with smart tech, it delivers effortless shifting and aggressive performance in every curve. With updated styling, refined electronics, and uncompromised control, the MT-09 Y-AMT redefines what it means to ride free.


Aggressive next-generation styling takes the Hyper Naked look to a new level with sharper, more defined lines and minimal bodywork. A bi-functional LED headlamp is shrouded in a cover, creating a compact and unified relationship with the fuel tank and tail section, while a more forward-leaning stance maintains the edgy street aesthetic synonymous with the MT name.


Yamaha's renowned 890cc liquid-cooled CP3 engine is light, powerful and provides a thrilling wave of torque from low rpms. A combination of carefully tuned induction sound and distinctive crossplane triple exhaust note provides a resonance which only heightens the feeling of acceleration. Throttle valves are governed by the Yamaha Chip Controlled Throttle (YCC-T) system enabling finer actuation and precise control.
